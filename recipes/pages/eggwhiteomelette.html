Spinach and Tomato Egg White Omelette

A delicious and delightful way to start the morning: With a healthy twist. This is what you need.

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients needed:
    Cooking Spray
  • 3 egg whites
  • salt
  • Ground Pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh spinach, or more to desired taste

    • Directions

      1.Heat a skillet over medium heat and spray with cooking spray.
      2.Whisk egg whites with salt and pepper in a bowl.
      3.Pour egg mixture into the heated skillet and swirl until eggs cover entire bottom of skillet. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
      4.Arrange spinach, tomatoes, and feta cheese in the middle of the eggs.
      5.Cook until edges of eggs begin to curl up, 2 to 3 more minutes. Loosen omelet from the skillet using a spatula and fold in half; continue cooking until cheese is melted, 2 to 3 more minutes.
