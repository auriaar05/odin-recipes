Fluffy Protein Pancakes

A healthy new way of beginning the morning is by starting with a new twist to pancakes: making it high protein. Thus, more filling and healthy than your traditional pancakes.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sweetener (entirely optional)
  • Protein powder
  • Greek yogurt
  • Baking powder

Directions:

  1. First, whisk in the eggs together with the vanilla extract, stevia, and Greek yogurt.
    2. Whisk in the protein powder. Then, whisk in the baking powder.
  2. Heat a nonstick griddle over medium heat and spray with oil.
    3. pour six to eight mounds of the batter into griddle.
  3. Cook pancakes over medium hear until they bubble on top. Then, flip and cook for only a few more seconds
  4. Remove the pancakes to a plate, add more fat as needed. Serve immediately.
